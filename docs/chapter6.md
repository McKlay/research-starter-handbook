**ðŸ”¹ Red Flags: Low-Quality or Predatory Venues**

Watch out for:

Predatory venues exist to profit from submission fees, not to advance science. They often lack rigorous peer review and may disappear after a few years. Publishing in such venues can harm your reputation and make your work difficult to find or cite.

- Spammy conference invites in your email
- Unrealistically **fast deadlines** or **guaranteed acceptance**
- **No peer review** or suspiciously short turnaround times
- Conference websites that look unprofessional or copy-pasted
- High **submission fees** with no real visibility

> âŒ If it feels like a diploma mill for papersâ€”it probably is.
